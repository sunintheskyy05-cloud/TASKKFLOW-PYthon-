-->> How to Use

Dashboard: Get a quick overview of your tasks and statistics
Kanban Board: Manage tasks visually by changing their status
New Task: Create detailed tasks with subtasks and due dates
Focus Timer: Use the Pomodoro timer to boost productivity (time is automatically logged)
Analytics: Export your weekly progress as CSV or beautifully formatted PDF
Settings: Switch between Dark/Light/System themes


🛠️ Technologies Used

GUI: CustomTkinter (Modern Tkinter)
Database: SQLite3
Data Handling: pandas
Reporting: ReportLab (PDF generation)
Logging: Python logging module
Configuration: JSON-based config
